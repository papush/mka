Up in the mountains of Bebedero (Escazú) there's a magical place called "la ventolera". At night, from the top of the hill the whole central valley can be appreciated with it's peculiar glowing web of artificial light.

Sheltered inside the woods we will set the camp, in between trees who protect us from the wind and any posibility of rain.
