Up in the mountains of Bebedero (Escazú) there's a place called "la ventolera", walking past this place we found a magical spot where the camp will be set. At night, from the top of the hill, the west central valley can be appreciated with it's peculiar glowing web of artificial light.

Sheltered within the woods we will set the camp, surrounded by trees who protect us from the wind and any posibility of rain.

On site, down into the creek there is a source of potable spring water

How to get there?

From San Jose:

  1. Take the bus to Bebedero (Escazu):

    The bus takes off from "Parque Ministerio de Salud", look it up on google maps.

    A bus takes off every 40 minutes. The last one takes off at 7:45pm, check out the schedule:

    BUS SCHEDULE

  2. Get off on the last stop and walk:

    On the last stop (in front of the church), walk the street that goes up the mountain heading west. It's a 3Km hike up to the location, 50 minutes by foot aprox. Keep your left, follow the red signals!

    When you get there you'll see a high red flag

    3. You can also use GPS navigation clicking the following button:

  3. Or ride on Don Hugo's pickup truck:

    Don Hugo is a local man who offers a ride from the bus stop to the location.

    He charges ₡1000 per person to carry you up the road directly to Mystika III.
