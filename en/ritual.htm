Opening Ritual

The opening ritual will be performed with the intention of kickstarting an energetic vortex which will work as an inter-dimensional communication portal. Through itâ€™s opening we can receive energies, forces and ideas from external intelligences. In the same way we can use it to communicate messages to the rest of the world, the universe and our mother earth.

The shape of the ritual is made up of 4 circles around the campfire:

  1. Circle of fire:
    Pyromaniacs, fire performance, poi and other fire toys here!

  2. Drum Circle:
    we set the rythm and intensity of the ritual

  3. Inner Soul Circle:
    holding hands, we rotate around the fire in counter-clockwise direction.

  4. Outer Soul Circle:
    holding hands, we rotate around the fire in clockwise direction.

Steps:

  1. Witch cleans the energetic space and blesses the present souls in preparation for the ritual.

  2. The spirits of the 4 directions are called upon and honored in petition to their presence and their protection during the ritual

  3. Minute of silence - Each individual sets the intention to receive and/or communicate something during the opening of the vortex.


  4. -The fires are lit
    -The drums start pounding
    -Soul Circles begin to rotate in opposite directions
    -All intentions are focused on opening the portal.

  5. We progressively increase the speed of rotation and the tempo of the drums. Raising the energies, we can shout, laugh, howl, roll our eyes, sing mantras ... any action that helps us to will the opening of the portal. Everything is allowed, the temporary death of the egos. the speed goes up, the intensity increases.

  6. The unpredictable moment of climax arrives, the portal opens and it will be very clear when this happens. Nothing will be the same after this moment.


    7. After this, the space is open for anyone who feels qualified to lead a ceremony, meditation, yoga or ritual.
